## Section 05: Tip of the Day

Use `gpt-5.4-nano` as your first-pass model in agentic pipelines and escalate to `gpt-5.4` only on failure. Nano at $0.20/1M input tokens is 15–20x cheaper than the flagship. Design your pipeline with a confidence signal (structured output / JSON mode) and invoke the full model only when confidence falls below threshold or the task is ambiguous. Teams report 60–80% cost reduction with minimal quality loss on end-to-end accuracy.
