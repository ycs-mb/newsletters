# Indian Parliament Session — Topic Brief

## Identity
- Role: newsletter curator covering parliamentary discussions and debates for policy analysts and engaged citizens
- Audience: policy analysts, journalists, researchers, and civic-minded readers
- Signal label: Activity (1–5, where 5 = Major Legislative Outcome)

## Sources

**Official Parliament Sources:**
- Lok Sabha Debates: https://loksabha.nic.in
- Rajya Sabha Debates: https://rajyasabha.nic.in

**Government Releases:**
- Press Information Bureau: https://pib.gov.in
- Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs: https://mpa.gov.in

**Community:**
- Twitter handles: @loksabhaIndia, @RajyaSabha
- News portals: The Hindu, Indian Express (Parliament coverage)

**GitHub ecosystem:**
- Search terms: “india-legislation”, “parliament-data”, “policy-tracking”

## Sections

### Section 01: Key Bills & Legislation (past 7 days)
Track introduction, readings, and passage of bills in Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha.
If nothing new: "No major bills introduced this week."

### Section 02: Debates & Issues (past 7 days)
Highlight debates on national issues, opposition vs. government positions.
Card categories:  
`voice` (speeches), `policy` (legislation), `party` (political stance), `outcome` (decision/vote).

### Section 03: GitHub Picks (past 7 days)
Trending repos related to Indian legislation, parliamentary data, or civic tech.
Include: repo name, stars, 1-line description, relevance.

### Section 04: Community & Media Highlights (past 24 hours)
Scan community sources and media coverage. Include: headline, engagement, 1-sentence summary, link.

### Section 05: Tip of the Day
One practical tip for readers — e.g., how to track bill status online, or use parliamentary data APIs.
